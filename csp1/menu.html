Tuna Sandwich Cake

Enter the tuna sandwich cake. The “cake” layers are made up of various breads, with fillings of vegetables, tuna and savory cream cheese “icing” between each layer.

Catpuccino

Php 100.00

The cafe's bestseller! Prepared with 1/3 part espresso, 1/3 part steamed milk and 1/3 part foam in a small catpuccino cup which are typically 4-6 oz. in capacity. Catpuccino can be garnished with a light sprinkle of ground chocolate, cocoa powder, cinnamon, or nutmeg.

Latte

Php 100.00 for two!

Our latte is a coffee-based drink made primarily from espresso and steamed milk. It consists of one-third espresso, two-thirds heated milk and about 1cm of foam. With our skilled baristas, the foam can be poured in such a way to create pictures of your favorite cats. Now buy one - take one! Purrfect!

Parfait

Php 100.00

Our parfait is made by layering parfait cream, ice cream, and flavored gelatins in a tall, clear glass. The cat shaped toppings are created with whipped cream, marshmallows, and fresh strawberries.

Cat Cookies

Php 20.00

Our little cat cookies are made with sweet dough, baked in single-sized servings and can be eaten out-of-hand. Cute!
