●Cats●

Doug

The man himself! Doug is a big teddy bear - and one of the eldest cat in the café. He eats exactly the same food as the other cats, but weighs in at 7.9kg! He's just made that way - his paws say it all. Doug is very lazy and doesn't like to be fussed for too long. But if you leave him in peace, he will chill with you for hours. He likes to hang out.

Lyn

Our beautiful girl! The first thing you notice about Lyn are her lovely eyes and striking asymmetrical markings, but get to know her and you'll also discover she's clumsy and awkward and very loving.

Rhé

There isn't a person who comes to the café who doesn't know who Rhé is. Our Rhé demands all the attention, all the time, and she will shout until she gets it. She loves the running wheel and can be very possessive of it. You have to watch her though, because if you don't watch her and praise her while she exercises, she loses all motivation!

Jill

Jill is cheeky and loving and full of beans! Jill loves to steal toys from other cats, lick people on the nose and dip her paws in your water glass while you're not looking. She is full of antics and mischief!

Nini

Nini sure gives Jill a run for her money in the mischief stakes! Nini loves to chase toys and scale the floor-to-ceiling scratcher. When she's not bounding around, you'll find her casually leaning a paw off a high shelf, watching the people pass by below.

Mio

Our soulful little man! No matter how big Mio gets, he still seems like a kitten to us. The gentlest spirit and the softest fur, he's an angel on earth.
